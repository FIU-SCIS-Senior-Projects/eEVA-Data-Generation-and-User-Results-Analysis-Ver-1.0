The Stages of Change Readiness and Treatment Eagerness Scale (SOCRATES) is an experimental instrument designed to assess readiness for change in alcohol abusers. Motivation is the driving force behind actions. Typically, motivation is described as being either intrinsic or extrinsic. With intrinsic motivation the individual will do things because they feel that it is good or right. Extrinsic motivation comes from societal expectations, peer groups and authority figures. Therefore an individual might be motivated to do something because they feel it is right or because they feel forced to by external conditions and expectations.
